Classic Cheesecake


A rich and creamy cheescake that fills your mouth with everlasting joy. Not as dense as New York Cheesecake, but is most certainly divine.

    Ingredients
  • Four 8-ounce bricks of cream cheese
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons Lemon essence
  • 3 Eggs

    Instructions
  1. Beat the above ingredients together in a bowl until white creamy substance is present. Ensure they are also at room temperature to prevent chunkiness.
  2. Buy a Graham Cracker crust and press it in your tin, until base is fully covered.
  3. Cover the side of your tin in foil.
  4. Place Your tin inside a water filled baking tray.
  5. Preheat your oven for 5 minutes minimum or until hot.
  6. Place tray inside your oven and set a timer for an hour.
  7. Monitor top of cheesecake for a golden brown look.
  8. Take tray out and let it cool for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Sit back; relax and enjoy!
